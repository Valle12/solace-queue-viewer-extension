This Privacy Policy describes how the Solace Queue Viewer Extension collects, uses, and discloses your information.

Information Collected

The Extension collects the following information to connect to your Solace PubSub+ broker and display messages:

- Solace Credentials: The Extension uses your in-process credentials (username, password, message vpn and secured smf host) to establish a secure connection to your Solace broker. These credentials are never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

The Extension uses the collected information solely for the purpose of connecting to your Solace broker and displaying messages within a chromium based browser. We do not:

- Share your credentials or connection details with any third party
- Track your browsing activity or collect any information about the websites you visit
- Sell or otherwise monetize your information

Security

We take steps to ensure the security of your information:

- We use industry-standard security practices to protect your credentials
- Your credentials and connection details are stored securely within your browser and are never transmitted to our servers

Open Source

The Solace Queue Browser is an open-source project available on GitHub: https://github.com/Valle12/solace-queue-viewer-extension. You can review the source code to verify our commitment to data privacy.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
